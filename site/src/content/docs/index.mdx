My Own Suite is a curated collection of open-source tools that are strong, practical alternatives to enterprise SaaS from Big Tech.

Instead of piecing everything together manually, you get a bundled stack with deployment automation and a clear path to ownership of your own data. The goal is simple: make open-source independence realistic for people with limited technical experience, without sacrificing quality.
