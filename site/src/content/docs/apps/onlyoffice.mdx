OnlyOffice provides document editing inside your browser. In this stack, it is connected to Seafile so files can be edited directly from your storage.

## What it does

- Creates and edits text documents
- Creates and edits spreadsheets
- Creates and edits presentations

## Dependencies in the stack

- Depends on: Seafile integration to open and save files from your library
- Used by: people who need collaborative office documents
