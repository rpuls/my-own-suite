Immich is planned as the photo and video layer for My Own Suite. It is designed to replace mainstream cloud photo backups while keeping your media private.

## What it does

- Backs up photos and videos from your devices
- Organizes media into a searchable private library
- Shares albums with people you choose

## Dependencies in the stack

- Depends on: none
- Status: roadmap app (not yet included in live deployment targets)
