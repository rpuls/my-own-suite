Seafile is the cloud storage layer in My Own Suite. It keeps your files under your control while still giving you modern sync and sharing features.

## What it does

- Stores files in your own private cloud
- Syncs files between desktop and mobile devices
- Shares files and folders with selected people

## Dependencies in the stack

- Depends on: none
- Works with: OnlyOffice for in-browser document editing
