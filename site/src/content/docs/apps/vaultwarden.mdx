Vaultwarden is your self-hosted password manager. It stores your passwords, passkeys, and secure notes so you can sign in safely across devices.

## What it does

- Saves passwords and fills them in when needed
- Stores secure notes and credentials
- Supports shared vaults for families or teams

## Dependencies in the stack

- Depends on: none
- Used by: all users who want secure login management
