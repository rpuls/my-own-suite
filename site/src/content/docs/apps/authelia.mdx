Authelia adds a familiar sign-in step in front of the suite surfaces that do not have their own built-in login wall. In My Own Suite it protects Homepage and Suite Manager, so users can reach the dashboard and onboarding flow through one shared account instead of hunting for bootstrap tokens or public URLs.
