Stirling PDF is your self-hosted PDF workspace. It gives you common document utilities without uploading files to third-party services.

## What it does

- Merges, splits, and rearranges PDFs
- Converts documents and images to PDF formats
- Runs OCR and other PDF processing tools locally

## Dependencies in the stack

- Depends on: none
- Used by: people who need day-to-day PDF workflows under their own control
