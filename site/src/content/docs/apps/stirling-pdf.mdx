Stirling PDF is your self-hosted PDF workspace. It gives you common document utilities without uploading files to third-party services.

## What it does

- Merges, splits, and rearranges PDFs
- Converts documents and images to PDF formats
- Runs OCR and other PDF processing tools locally

## Dependencies in the stack

- Depends on: none
- Used by: people who need day-to-day PDF workflows under their own control

## Persistence

- Mount a persistent volume at `/configs` to keep users and settings across restarts/redeploys.
- If your platform supports more than one volume, optional paths are:
  - `/pipeline`
  - `/usr/share/tessdata`
  - `/logs`
  - `/customFiles`
- If your deployment platform supports only one persistent volume per service, use `/configs` as the priority mount.

## Health check

- Recommended HTTP health endpoint: `/api/v1/info/status`
